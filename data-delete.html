HasalHype - Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users of HasalHype can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the HasalHype mobile app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.
If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by contacting support at: support@hasalhype.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile information (e.g. name, email, phone number)
  • Transaction and payment-related data
  • Device information and system logs associated with your account

Data Retention

All user data is scheduled for permanent deletion within 30 days of receiving your request. After this period, your data cannot be recovered.
